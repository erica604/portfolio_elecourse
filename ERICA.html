"The 27th king of Silla was King Seondeok Women's University, whose last name was Kim and his father was King Jinpyeong. It is a historical fact that Queen Seondeok ascended the throne. This happened in the past. So history means "facts in the past." So how do we know what happened in the past that's already gone. That's because there's a record of the past. If you look at books such as "The History of the Three Kingdoms" and "The History of the Three Kingdoms," you can see many historical facts that occurred during the Silla period.
